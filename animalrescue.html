User Research

User Flow Redesign

Information Architecture

STAR's mission is to help homeless and unwanted animals within the Philadelphia area. In this project, I took research to the users and the website, redesigned the user flow of adoption. The new product improved the adoption experience of adopters and provide a better service system for the organization.

PERSONA

The main user of the STAR are people who want to adopt a pet. I created a persona based on the interview of people who has adoption experience.

USER JOURNEY

When people trying to adopt a pet on STAR, there are three low points. Each low point due to a problem that we can solve.

1

The adoption process is too complicated.

2

Inconvenient to share the detail of pet to friends and families.

3

Always forget the day of vaccination.

To solve the biggest problem, I need to improve the adoption pages so that users can finish the adoptions easier and quicker.

CONTENT

Link:Excel of All Content

I reorganized the content of website and devided them as four main sections. To improve the adoption experience relate to the section of "To adopt". The main content are adoption animal informations and adoption files that adopters need to provide.

CURRENT USERFLOW

In current page, users need to record pet's ID to fill the adoption form, download all adoption files, print forms, call or message people for references, take all files to the organization, back to home and wait fro the approvement. After the call back from the STAR, adopters can finally take the pet back to home.

IMPROVED USERFLOW

In the improved user flow, adopters just need to fill all forms online, share the link to get the reference, the only thing left to do is wait for the permission from the organization. People can check the adoption status online easily.
