DESCRIPTION

BACKGROUND

The Silent Shield is a brewery run by a Scottish Woman whose family has brewed beer, ale, and spirits for centuries. She is looking to bring this rich history to the states with a physical location offering in-house brewed craft beer and great food.

GOAL

Build a website which can show history of brewery and craft of beer to attract customers.

INTERVIEW

I did the interview to both customers and the brewery.

SITEMAP

WIREFRAME

I started build the wireframe by the page to show the details of beers. This is also the most important page. Begin with the mobile page and then I expanded them to webpages.

1. Hamburger bar to show the navigation.

2. Big image of beer or food to attract customers.

3. Show the tastes and recipe in details.

4. Show customers review on the page as references.

5. Jump to any page by the footer very quick.

MOODBOARD

Due to the handmade craft, I used a lot of handpainted elements, and I used some british elements like scotland grids.

BRANDING

Brand Color

I picked some color from the Scotland flag as the brand color.

Logo

I combined the shape of beer bottle with the shield, and use the pattern of scotland grid as the logo.

Typography

I used handwriting style font in the pages.
